Best results checklist

Before you hit start, keep these in mind.

  • Use a well-lit space so MediaPipe can lock onto your hand quickly.
  • Center one hand in frame and keep it about 40cm from the camera.
  • Pause between letters when using single-shot mode for higher confidence.
  • Use the history panel to confirm predictions before copying the transcript.

Best results checklist

Before you hit start, keep these in mind.

  • Use a well-lit space so MediaPipe can lock onto your hand quickly.
  • Center one hand in frame and keep it about 40cm from the camera.
  • Pause between letters when using single-shot mode for higher confidence.
  • Use the history panel to confirm predictions before copying the transcript.

Operator tips for silky-smooth demos.

Save these quick notes before you walk into your next pitch or usability session.

Lighting cue

Diffuse lamps or daylight from the side helps the landmarks lock on instantly.

Framing guide

Keep elbows off frame and hold your palm 35–45cm away for the sharpest crops.

Gesture pacing

Pause for 1 beat between letters in single-shot mode to avoid blended predictions.

Shortcut friendly

Use the copy + clear buttons to reset the transcript between participants.
